Privacy Policy — Office Breaker

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Office Breaker is developed by Niv Mor. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Office Breaker mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, and optionally your name and company name.

Workout Data: We collect data about your workout sessions including session duration, exercises completed, streaks, and points earned.

App Preferences: We store your app settings such as language preference, notification settings, sound settings, and schedule preferences.

Social Features: If you enable group workouts, your name and company may be visible to colleagues using the same company email domain. This is opt-in only.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the Office Breaker service
  • Track your workout progress and streaks
  • Send workout reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • Enable social features such as Break Buddies (only if you opt in)
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google) cloud services, which use industry-standard encryption and security practices. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Social Features and Data Sharing

The social features of Office Breaker are strictly opt-in. Your name and company will only be visible to other users if you explicitly enable "Join group workouts" in your profile settings. You can disable this at any time.

5. Notifications

We send push notifications to remind you of scheduled workouts. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings or within the app under Settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of social features at any time
  • Disable notifications at any time

To delete your account, go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account within the app, or contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Office Breaker is intended for adults in workplace settings. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
