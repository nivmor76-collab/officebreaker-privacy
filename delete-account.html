אופיס ברייקר · How to delete your account and all associated data

Office Breaker gives you full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data in two ways.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Office Breaker app.
  2. Go to Profile (פרופיל).
  3. Open Settings (הגדרות).
  4. Tap Delete Account (מחיקת חשבון) and confirm.

Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us and we will delete your account and data on your behalf:

Email: nivmor76@gmail.com

Please send the request from the email address associated with your Office Breaker account, or include it in the message, so we can verify and locate your account. Requests are handled within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes: your account and login, your workout history, points, streaks and rank, your app preferences, and your profile and community data (name, company, group-workout participation). This action cannot be undone.

For details on how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy.
